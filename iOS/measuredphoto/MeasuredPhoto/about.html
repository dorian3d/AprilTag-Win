This app uses RealityCap's 3DK library, featuring Visual-Intertial Metrology (VIM) technology. VIM combines computer vision and inertial data to produce accurate 6DOF device motion and a 3D point cloud of the environment. App developers can use 3DK in their own apps to bring the power of VIM to fields such as measurement, augmented reality, and indoor navigation. For more information, visit http://realitycap.com.
